# SUMO

## Overview

**SUMO: Suggested Upper Merged Ontology**

webpage:
* https://www.ontologyportal.org/
> The Suggested Upper Merged Ontology (SUMO) and its domain ontologies form the largest formal public ontology in existence today.

GitHub:
* https://github.com/ontologyportal/sumo
* all assets are in `.kif` format, however; and it's not clear whether/how `.kif` can be converted to OWL

SUMO includes MILO (Mid-Level Ontology)
> an ontology used to bridge the abstract content of the SUMO with the rich detail of domain ontologies

Download:
* https://github.com/abrahaj/SUMO.owl
> A provisional and necessarily lossy translation [of SUMO `.kif`] to OWL
* `SUMO.owl` (37MB)
